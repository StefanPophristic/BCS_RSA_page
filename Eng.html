Interactive Image Naming Study

You are invited to participate in an online study. During the study, you will play a ~30 min interactive online game where you will chat with another person in order to identify images. You will be paid $7 for your participation.

To participate, you must:

  • Speak English as a native language and be able to write and read in English
  • Be over 18 years old
  • Live in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Spain, or Italy

To begin the game:

  1. Find another person who fulfills the above criteria and can play the game with you. This can be, for example, a friend or family member.
  2. Play the game on a different computer and different room than your partner. You will both be paid for your participation.
  3. Press the link below at the same time as your partner to begin the game:

https://crossling-ref.meteorapp.com/?batchGroupName=EngCS

Both you and your partner will receive $7 for your participation through an Amazon gift card. If you are not located in the US, you will receive the equivalent of 7 USD in your local currency.

If you have any questions or encounter technical problems while taking the study, please contact Stefan Pophristic (stefpop@stanford.edu).

Thank you for participating!
